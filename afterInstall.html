After installing "IEToolsChrome", you also need to install our Google Chrome Extension from the Chrome Webstore. Until it's published, use the steps below...
  1. Launch Google Chrome.
  2. Navigate to chrome://extensions/. Click the radio button to enable "Developer mode" (which enables side-loading).
  3. Click on the button "Load unpacked". Naavigate to NativeMessaging\Extension and click on "Select Folder".
  4. After the extension is loaded, you'll see "Cerner Tools" on the extension page & also an icon will appear in the Chrome header.

You can hide the icon in the Chrome header if you want. You can ignore the error: Unchecked runtime.lastError: Could not establish connection. Receiving end does not exist.

Every time you start Chrome, you'll be warned about side loaded extensoins.
